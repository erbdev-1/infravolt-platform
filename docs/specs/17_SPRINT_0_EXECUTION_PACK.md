## 10. Working Agreement

1. One decision, one owner.
2. Unknown becomes Blocked, not assumed.
3. Evidence link required for Done.
4. Asset filename is not licence evidence.
5. Product image/copy is not technical proof.
6. Ukraine is not deferred translation cleanup.
7. No real secrets in documents/messages.
8. Task scope changes through Head Agent.
9. Daily blocker visibility.
10. Day 10 gate is pass, conditional pass or fail.

## 103. G0 Outcomes

### Pass

- score at least 85,
- no unresolved critical owner/scope/repository/domain architecture blocker,
- Sprint 1 tasks Ready.

### Conditional Pass

- score 70–84,
- remaining gaps have owners/dates,
- Sprint 1 work can proceed without assumption,
- affected publication features remain blocked.

### Fail

- below 70,
- release scope/owner absent,
- critical entity/domain/Gersan/rights decision would change foundation,
- Sprint 1 would require invention.

Score does not override a critical blocker.
